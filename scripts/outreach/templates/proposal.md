## How Pulse Works: Multi-Agent Architecture

Pulse uses a **multi-agent architecture** where specialized AI agents collaborate to manage distinct marketing functions autonomously while coordinating through a central orchestration layer.

### Agent 1: AI Marketing Strategist
**Purpose:** Strategic oversight and coordination
- Ingests brand guidelines, historical campaigns, and audience personas
- Defines campaign objectives and delegates tasks to execution agents
- Monitors KPIs and recommends strategy adjustments in real-time
- Ensures brand consistency across all automated outputs

### Agent 2: Social Media & Performance Analyst
**Purpose:** Content automation and unified analytics
- Schedules posts with AI-optimized timing based on engagement data
- Aggregates metrics from all platforms into one dashboard
- Detects anomalies and triggers automated alerts
- Generates predictive forecasts for campaign performance

### Agent 3: AI Customer Relationship Agent
**Purpose:** Intelligent audience engagement
- Consolidates DMs, comments, and messages into a unified inbox
- Generates brand-aligned response suggestions using LLMs
- Prioritizes urgent messages using sentiment analysis
- Handles voice notes through speech-to-text pipelines

### Agent 4: Content & Market Authority Engine
**Purpose:** Autonomous content generation and SEO optimization
- Monitors trending topics for content inspiration
- Generates platform-optimized post variants
- Validates brand consistency before publication
- Analyzes competitor content for strategic positioning
